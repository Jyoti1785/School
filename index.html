CSE Department

To attain a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering, a student must complete a minimum of 143 credits of course work which typically takes 4 years.

The courses of the CSE undergraduate degree is designed keeping two thing in mind:

The Computer Science and Engineering degree also provides students with an in-depth study of hardware to balance the software side.

Students are introduced to classical electrical circuits concepts through CSE104+L course.

Students are also introduced with FPGA based systems in advanced electronics courses such as CSE310+L.
